# Frequently Asked Questions

## General

### What is Marker for Creators?
Marker for Creators is a two-part tool: an iOS app for setting markers during a shoot or review, and a desktop Companion app (Mac and Windows) that exports those markers directly into your NLE of choice.

### Is there a Windows version of the Companion app?
Yes. Marker for Creators Companion is available for both Mac and Windows.

---

## Features and Compatibility

### Does the Companion support multicam clips?
**Multicam support is coming.** Currently the Companion handles single-camera timelines. When you have a multicam project and import it, clips are treated as individual clips rather than a multicam group. Proper multicam support, where clips are recognized and exported as multicam groups, is actively being developed.

### Which NLEs are supported?
- Final Cut Pro X
- DaVinci Resolve
- Adobe Premiere Pro
- Generic CSV / spreadsheet

### Can I use the app without the Companion?
Yes. The iOS app works standalone for note-taking and marker management. The Companion is only needed when you want to export markers into your NLE.
