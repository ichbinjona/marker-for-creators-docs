## v1.5.0

*March 2026*

### Main Features

- **The One-Click Editing Workflow** - Introducing the all-new Companion App for Mac and PC - built from the ground up with maximum time efficiency and simplicity in mind. No exports, no manual steps. Your markers sync instantly to the Companion App, your footage is found automatically, and one click opens everything in your editing software. Everything that can be automated, is automated.
- **Markers on Video Clips** - Markers are now placed directly onto your footage. Drag them anywhere - your edit comes together effortlessly.
- **Timeline Mode** - Build your timeline on set. The Companion App places video clips automatically - so editing can start the second you wrap.
- **Editable Sessions** - Review markers while and after shooting. Edit notes, add or remove markers.
