# iOS App – Overview

**Marker for Creators** is an iOS app that lets you set markers, notes, and tags during shoots or while reviewing footage, directly on your iPhone or iPad. Everything syncs to the Companion app on your Mac or PC for seamless export into your NLE.

## What This Section Covers

- Interface walkthrough
- Creating and editing markers
- Marker types and colors
- Session management
- Syncing with the Companion app
- Export options from iOS
