# Quick Start

Get from zero to markers in your editing timeline in under 5 minutes.

## Step 1: Install the Apps

1. Download **Marker for Creators** on your [iPhone from the App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/marker-for-creators/id6755014404).
2. Download the **Companion App** on your Mac or PC from [markerforcreators.com/companion](https://markerforcreators.com/companion).

## Step 2: Create an Account

Open the Marker app on your iPhone and sign in. You can create a free account with your email. Use the same account to sign into the Companion App on your computer.

Your sessions will sync automatically between both apps.

## Step 3: Set Up Your Camera

Go to **Settings > How to set up your camera** and select your camera brand and model. Follow the step-by-step instructions to enable timecode on your camera.

::: tip Why this matters
Marker uses timecode to place your markers at exactly the right position in your footage. Getting this right takes one minute and saves you hours.
:::

## Step 4: Start a Session

Open the Marker app and tap the red **Start Session** button. Choose your session type:

- **Time-of-Day** - matches your camera's real-world timecode. Best for multi-camera setups and most workflows.
- **Rec Run** - counts up from 00:00:00. Only for single-clip recordings.

## Step 5: Set Markers While Filming

When something important happens, tap one of the four marker buttons. That's it - a marker is placed at the current timecode.

- **Tap** = point marker
- **Hold** = In/Out range marker

## Step 6: Import into Your Editing Software

1. Open the **Companion App** on your Mac or PC.
2. Sign in with the same account.
3. Your session appears automatically.
4. Click the **Play** button next to your session.
5. Your editing software opens with markers already in place.

Done. You just saved yourself hours of scrubbing through footage.
