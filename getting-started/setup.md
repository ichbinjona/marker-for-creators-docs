# Setup Guide

Everything you need to configure before your first shoot.

## Installing the Apps

### iPhone App

Download **Marker for Creators** from the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/marker-for-creators/id6755014404). The app works on iPhone and iPad.

### Companion App

Download the **Companion App** for Mac or Windows from [markerforcreators.com/companion](https://markerforcreators.com/companion). The Companion turns your markers into native projects for your editing software.

## Creating Your Account

Open the Marker app on your iPhone and sign in with your email. Then sign into the Companion App with the same credentials. Your sessions sync automatically between devices in real-time.

## Choosing Your Editing Software

In the iPhone app, go to **Settings** and select your preferred editing software:

- **DaVinci Resolve**
- **Premiere Pro**
- **Final Cut Pro**

This determines which marker colors are available and what format the Companion App exports.

::: info
You can always change this later. The Companion App can also convert sessions between editing software formats at any time.
:::

## Setting Up Your Camera

Go to **Settings > How to set up your camera**. Select your camera brand and model to see step-by-step instructions for enabling timecode.

Marker supports cameras from Sony, Canon, Blackmagic, ARRI, and many more. The camera database is regularly updated.

### Time-of-Day Timecode

For the best experience, set your camera to **Time-of-Day** timecode mode. This means your camera records the actual time of day into each video file. Marker uses this to match your markers to the correct frame in your footage - no manual syncing required.

### Important: Check Your Camera Date

Make sure the **date and time on your camera are correct**. The Companion App uses the recording date to find and match your footage automatically. If your camera's clock is off, automatic matching may not work properly.

::: tip Daily Timecode Check
Marker reminds you to check your camera's timecode before your first shoot of the day. Spending 10 seconds on this can save you from sync issues later.
:::

## Setting Up Apple Watch (Optional)

Install **Marker for Creators** on your Apple Watch via the Watch app on your iPhone. Once a session is running on your iPhone, your Watch shows the same four marker buttons - perfect for marking without reaching for your phone.

→ [Learn more about the Apple Watch feature](/features/apple-watch)

## Import Your Session

1. Open the **Companion App** on your Mac or PC
2. Sign in with the same Marker account
3. Make sure your footage is on your computer (or your external drives are connected)
4. Select your session and press **Play**
5. Your editing software opens with all markers and footage in place

→ [Learn more about the Companion App](/companion-app/)
