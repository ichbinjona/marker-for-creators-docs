**Marker for Creators Companion** is the desktop app for Mac and Windows that takes your markers from the iOS app and turns them into native markers in your editing software.

## Supported export formats

- Final Cut Pro X (`.fcpxml`)
- DaVinci Resolve (`.drt`, `.otio`)
- Adobe Premiere Pro (`.xml`, `.csv`)
- Generic CSV / spreadsheet export
